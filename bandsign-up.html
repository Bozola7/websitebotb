Band signup

When applying to join the Battle of the Bands competition, bands must provide their reason for joining; what their band name is; details on thier band members, such as names and ages. In addition the band will need to provide their contact details (Phone number and/or e-mail address) so that we are able to contact them about whether or not they are in the competition, and also provide them with updates of important information about the competition.
