ACDC is made out of four members, and has been playing music since 1973. Will their experience help them win this Battle of the Bands?

Black Sabbath

Black Sabbath are made up of five members, these being Ozzy Osbourne as lead vocalist; Tony Iommi as lead guitarist; Geezer Butler as bass guitarist; Tommy Clufetos on drums and Adam Wakerman on keyboard.
