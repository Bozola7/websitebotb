Festival tickets

Standard Ticket - 19.99

Through purchasing this ticket you will have access to all the main facilities of the festival, such as the food & drink stands, clothing, make-up & jewellery stands.

Front-Row Ticket - 49.99

Through purchasing this ticket you gain all the benefits from the "Standard Ticket", whilst having access to the front row area of the festival, creating a more atmospheric experience for yourself.

VIP Ticket - 89.99

To optimise your experience at the festival purchase the VIP ticket, and you will gain all the benefits from the "Standard Ticket" and the "Front-row Ticket", whilst being able to go backstage and meet with all of the band members from your favourite bands.
